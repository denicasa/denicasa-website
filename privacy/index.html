For the purposes of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), Denisa Fussiova is the data controller.

2. What Personal Data We Collect

We may collect the following information when you contact us or use enquiry forms:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number / WhatsApp number
  • Property preferences (budget, locations, bedrooms, etc.)
  • Any other information you include in your message

We do not intentionally collect special category (sensitive) personal data.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Respond to enquiries and provide requested information
  • Arrange viewings and follow up with relevant listings
  • Provide mortgage guidance if requested
  • Improve the website and service quality
  • Comply with legal obligations where applicable

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following lawful bases:

  • Consent (when you submit an enquiry form or request contact)
  • Legitimate interest (responding to enquiries and providing requested services)
  • Legal obligation (where required under applicable Spanish/EU regulations)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your data only where necessary to respond to your request, such as with:

  • Developers / agents (to arrange viewings or provide information)
  • Mortgage partners or lenders (only if you request mortgage support)
  • Legal / professional advisers (if required for a transaction)

6. Data Storage & Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your data. Data may be processed using third-party services (for example, Google services used for contact form processing).

7. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary to handle your enquiry, provide services, or meet legal obligations. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 8).

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to processing
  • Request data portability

To exercise these rights, email denisa@denicasa.es. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Authority (AEPD).

9. Cookies

This website may use essential cookies required for basic functionality. If analytics or marketing cookies are added in the future, this policy will be updated and (where required) consent will be requested.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.
